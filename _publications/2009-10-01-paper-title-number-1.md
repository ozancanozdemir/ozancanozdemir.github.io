## Overview

This paper investigates how COVID-19 lockdown measures affected **PM10** and **SO2** concentrations across Turkey, and how these changes were associated with shifts in **human mobility**. The study combines environmental indicators with mobility data to better understand the relationship between policy restrictions, movement patterns, and air quality.
