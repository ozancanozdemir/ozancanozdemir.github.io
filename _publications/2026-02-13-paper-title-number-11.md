## Overview

This preprint studies the detection of **AI-generated content in Turkish news media** using a fine-tuned Turkish BERT classifier. It moves beyond self-reported perceptions of AI use in journalism and offers an empirical, data-driven approach to measuring AI-rewritten content across Turkish news outlets.
