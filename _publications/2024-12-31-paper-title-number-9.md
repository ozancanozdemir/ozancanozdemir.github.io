## Overview

This article applies **statistical and machine learning-based methods** to forecast drought phenomena in the **Central Anatolia Region of Turkey**. It connects climate science, forecasting, and applied machine learning, showing how data-driven methods can support the analysis of environmental risk and regional drought dynamics.
