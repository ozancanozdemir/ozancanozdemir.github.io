## Overview

This article examines how **ensemble** and **hybrid forecasting tools** can improve predictive accuracy through a Turkey COVID-19 case study. It contributes to applied forecasting research by comparing model combinations and highlighting the value of hybrid approaches in complex, rapidly changing public health settings.
